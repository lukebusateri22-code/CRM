# ⚡ Workflows Page - Complete Guide

## 🎯 What Are Workflows?

**Workflows are automation rules that save you hours of manual work.** They automatically perform actions when specific events happen in your CRM.

**Think of it as:** "When X happens, automatically do Y"

## 🚀 How to Use

### **View All Workflows:**
1. Navigate to "Workflows" in the menu
2. See statistics at the top
3. Browse all workflows below

### **Filter by Category:**
1. Click "Sales", "Marketing", or "Operations"
2. See only workflows in that category
3. Click "All" to see everything

### **Enable/Disable a Workflow:**
1. Click the Play/Pause button
2. Green = Active, Gray = Paused
3. Toast notification confirms the change

### **Duplicate a Workflow:**
1. Click the blue Copy button
2. New workflow appears with "(Copy)" suffix
3. Edit and customize as needed

### **Delete a Workflow:**
1. Click the red Trash button
2. Workflow is removed
3. Toast confirms deletion

### **Create from Template:**
1. Click "Templates" button
2. Browse 6 pre-built workflows
3. Click any template to create it
4. Workflow appears in your list (paused)
5. Enable it when ready
